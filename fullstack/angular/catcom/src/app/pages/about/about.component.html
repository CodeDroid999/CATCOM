St veronica

When Christ was walking to Calvary, his face dripping with sweat and blood, Saint Veronica, a bystander was moved with compassion. She approached Jesus and offered Him a cloth, likely her veil, which He accepted and used to wipe His face. The image of His face was subsequently imprinted on the cloth.Saint Veronica feast day is 12th July and she is the patron saint of maids, parsonage housekeepers, photographers, rectory housekeepers, seamstress, washerwomen, against bleeding, against hemorrhages, domestic workers, dying people, laundry workers and linen weavers.

Our Choir, KMRM

Kwaya ya Mtakatifu Romano Mtunzi

Evangelizing through music, Six albums and counting...
Our story

Initially identified as JKUAT Catcom Choir, it was renamed to KMRM in 2022 after the patron of music: Saint Romanus the Melodist. The choir manages a YouTube channel where they upload song performances and albums with latest one, Volume six launched in February 2021 with other projects on the way. The choir also offer music classes to the choir members and other interested persons at a fee.

The choir is building the KMRM Studio which will not only serve the choir but the catholic community, the St. Augustine Parish and other interested parties for audio and video productions. The family of approximately 100 makes every second mass at St. Augustine Centre lively as ever.We meet at 6pm of every friday at SPA106 and every Saturday at 3pm at FABS for a 2 hour practice session. All are welcome!

You can support the choir by subscribing to the KMRM youtube channel.
