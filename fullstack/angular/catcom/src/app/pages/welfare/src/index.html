COMMUNITY WELL-BEING

Promoting community well-being within CATCOM and working towards a supportive and nurturing environment

Our activities

Welfare team

Our objectives
Offer assistance towards the well being of CatCom student members suffering due to lack of basic needs especially food
Work together to serve the community and promote the welfare of the members, holistically, that is spiritually, promoting mental health and financially
Reach out to those students who are in dire challenges and at a point of dismay in their undertakings during the semester

Our projects

20forchange project

This is a project where community members through their SCCs each contribute 20 shillings in aid of the welfare movement. This kitty goes out to support the needy students in the community and to other projects as directed by the CATCOM executive

Misalets project

The welfare team sells misalets and mass booklets before mass on Wednesdays and on Sunday during the second mass at the Parish site. The misalets retails at 25/= The misalets contains the missal readings for the day and the liturgical order to be followed during the mass. Support us by buying our misalets!
